# Sales Pipeline Insights

This project answers a revenue leadership question: which pipeline motions are creating reliable revenue, where are bottlenecks forming, and what revenue should the business expect over the next six months?

## Business Questions

1. How much total and weighted pipeline exists by stage?
2. Which sales reps are converting pipeline into booked revenue most effectively?
3. Which lead sources produce the best win rates and deal quality?
4. Which account segments move fastest through the sales cycle?
5. What revenue is likely over the next six months using historical trend plus weighted open pipeline?

## Analytical Approach

- Generated a realistic CRM opportunity dataset with lead source, stage, segment, rep, deal value, expected close date, and actual close results.
- Loaded the raw data into SQLite to practice SQL joins, aggregation, conditional metrics, and KPI validation.
- Exported dashboard-ready CSV files for funnel analysis, rep performance, lead-source analysis, segment analysis, and monthly revenue.
- Built a six-month revenue forecast using a linear trend from recent won revenue blended with weighted open pipeline by expected close month.

## Recommended Resume Bullets

- Built an end-to-end sales pipeline analytics project using Python, SQLite, SQL, and JavaScript to analyze 2,600 CRM opportunities across funnel stage, lead source, account segment, and sales rep.
- Created KPI outputs for win rate, weighted pipeline, booked revenue, sales cycle length, source quality, and rep performance, then converted results into an executive dashboard.
- Developed a six-month revenue forecast by blending historical revenue trends with stage-weighted open pipeline to support revenue planning.
